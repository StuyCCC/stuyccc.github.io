PClassic Spring 2023 Signups

PClassic Signups

The PClassic competition will take place in-person on April 22, 2023 (Saturday), at the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, PA.

This will be a one-day trip from Stuyvesant on a chartered bus. Departure and return times are not yet certain however expect a morning departure around 8am-9am from Stuyvesant, and returning back to the city around 7pm-10pm.

Based on current budget estimates, there will be no cost or payment required to go on the trip, with funds coming from a SU allocation to StuyCCC. Lunch should be covered by the orgnizers. Breakfast and dinner will not be provided so you should bring your own.

Due to limitations with the size of the bus and chaperone requirements, we can only accommodate a maximum of 45-50 students.

Signups are NOT first-come-first-served. In case too many students sign up, we will consider factors such as ability, CCC meeting attendance (if you are a member), and team size (discouraging 1 person teams) to see who gets to go.

Teams can have anywhere from 1-4 members, however teams of 1 person are strongly discouraged and receive lower priority in case too many students sign up, so you can enter your team on the form if you have already decided on one. If you wish to be assigned a team depending on availability, there is an option for that on the form.

There will be two divisions: Classic (formerly Novice) and Advanced (formerly Standard). If your team is composed of people new to CS, we'd recommend choosing the novice division. If you have experience coding and think you can solve a couple problems 5-8, we'd recommend participating in the standard division.

The only three languages you are allowed to compete in are Python, Java, and C++.

The PClassic website is here. You can find the rules, more infomration about logistics, as well as past problem sets, at their website.

Only one person from each team needs to fill the signup form out.In case of any changes to the form, or special circumstances (eg. you having your own transportation) please email us.

The signup form with more detailed info is available here, signups are tentatively due April 8, 2023. More information will come later on, either fill out the signup form or get the @Ping role on our Discord to be notified of updates, further logistics information, and paperwork. READ THE INSTRUCTIONS FULLY ON THE FORM BEFORE FILLING IT OUT, THERE ARE SOME DIFFERENCES WITH THE FORM'S INSTRUCTIONS AND THIS ONE HERE.

In addition, PLEASE HELP US ADVERTISE. Tell anyone who may be interested (such as your CS classes for example), there is no requirement that one is in StuyCCC to participate.

Lastly: due to NYCDOE chaperone requirements, we are looking for parent chaperones who can come with us on the trip. Please contact us (via email of Discord) ASAP.

PClassic

The PClassic competition takes place virtually on November 20th. It takes place from 10AM - 5PM, and we will take the competition in the Stuyvesant building.

Teams can have anywhere from 1-4 members, so you can enter your team at this google form if you have already decided on one. We also have a list of our members on StuyActivities, if you want to find people to ask.

There will be two division: Novice and Standard. If your team is composed of people new to CS, we'd recommend choosing the novice division. If you have experience coding and are up for a challenge, we'd recommend participating in the standard division.

The PClassic website is here. You can find the rules, as well as past problem sets to practice / gauge difficulty, at their website.
